About

Strategy meets execution.

My experience bridges strategy and operations. Before enrolling at Harvard, I spent eight years in consulting — leading engagements focused on improving performance rethinking work systems and how teams operate within them.

I've built new lines of revenue, managed teams, and partnered closely with executives to deliver change. That work taught me to move between the boardroom and the front line — to see problems as they're defined at the top and as they're lived day to day.

At HBS, my research focuses on how teams perform under pressure, examining the interpersonal and organizational dynamics that drive execution and problem solving. I enjoy immersing myself in the organizations I study, producing insights steeped in practice.

This combination of experience has honed my ability to diagnosis operational challenges and intervene productively — connecting how work is designed with how it actually gets done.
